  • Reading 1

    • Good afternoon everybody, my name is _________. I am going to talk about feelings, jobs and places. I am joyful. I am not scared. I am a professor. I am not a lawyer. I am at the shopping mall. I am not at the convenience store.

  • Reading 2

    • Good morning everybody, my name is _________. I am going to describe this picture. First, you can see a man. He is joyful. He is a hairdresser. He is at the convenience store. He has passion fruit juice in the morning. He likes blackberries. He doesn’t like coconuts. He wants cucumbers. He doesn’t want radishes. He sometimes drinks blackberry smoothie at the ice cream parlor. Thanks so much.

  • Reading 3

    • Good evening everybody, my name is _________. I am going to describe this picture. Then, you can see a woman. She is scared. She is an engineer. She is at the ice cream parlor. She has chicken and rice in the afternoon. She likes avocados. She doesn’t like papayas. She wants garlics. She doesn’t want broccolis. She usually eats chicken wings at the pizza place. She always washes the dishes in the morning. Thanks so much.
